Bio

I'm currently the Chief Technology Officer at Cloud Gold Pte. Ltd. in Singapore, where we build AI-Infra, AI-Gateway, and AI-SaaS products. Before joining Cloud Gold, I spent seven years leading NLP and dialogue systems at three of China's top mobility and automotive companies: Li Auto, XPeng Motors, and DiDi. I received my master's degree from the joint training program between the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where my research focused on deep learning and video captioning.
